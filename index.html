About Me

Hey there! Welcome to my personal little corner of the internet. This is my website, built with every ounce of effort and sweat, piece by piece. While it may still have some imperfections, I'm committed to continuous learning and improvement. I'm a second-year university student majoring in Electronic Information Science and Technology. Since the summer after my first year, I've developed a deep interest in computer science and have invested a lot of time in studying it. Currently, I'm delving into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and this website is a small project for me to test my learning progress. My dream is to become an excellent programmer, especially a full-stack engineer. I aspire to the digital nomad lifestyle, working freely around the globe and exploring different cultures and ways of life. At the same time, I hope to contribute my own efforts on platforms like GitHub one day, engaging with more tech experts for mutual learning and growth. Thank you for visiting!
